MISSION





Gaming is the defining bonding and entertainment experience for people of all ages, races, creeds, and locations. It fosters critical thinking skills, teamwork, and new friendships while strengthening existing ones.

Minnesports is Minnesota's first and only official E-Sports league. Our mission is to remain open to the public, unlike other E-Sports organizations which require sponsorships from academia or professional leagues.

All people of any age are welcome at our bring-your-own-rig open registration tournaments. Winners of the open registration tournaments are invited to compete in the Annual Cup for a grand prize, trophy, and bragging rights.

Open registration tournaments take place twice per year and registration begins two months prior. You'll be required to verify your personal information, sign a simple liability waiver (Your legal guardian can sign for you if you are under 18 years of age), and submit your PC specifications.
